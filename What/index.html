MIND YOUR MANNERS

A glipse of future commutes where courtesy is no longer contagious but mandated by etiquette programs enforced by artifical intelligence and surveillance.

What happens when camera surveillance is coupled with courtesy campaigns in our subway system? What if the MTA could offer a reduction in subway fare if subway riders are caught being "good subway citizens?" What originally may result in kinder, more empathetic subway rides, may quickly spiral into unwieldy behavior.
Four subway experiences are explored: A competition of "after you"; exact positioning behind the yellow line; what are the costs of taking a seat?; and as always, there are those who will not be controlled.


Michal Bacon

Design Methods - Fall 2017
